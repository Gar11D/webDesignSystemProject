Text Styles

Here are some text styles.

  • Font weight: 700 (bold)
  • Font-size: 26px
  • Font-family: Nunito Sans

H2: Subheading

  • Font weight: 500
  • Font-size: 18px
  • Font-family: Roboto

P: paragraph text

  • Font weight: 400 (regular)
  • Font-size: 14px
  • Font-family: Courier New
